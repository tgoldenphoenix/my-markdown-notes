# Equestrian terms

## Action

Horses can use various [gaits](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horse_gait) (dáng đi; patterns of leg movement) during locomotion across solid ground, either naturally or as a result of specialized training by humans.

gallop (phi nước đại)

The [trot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trot) is a two-beat diagonal horse gait where the diagonal pairs of legs move forward at the same time with a moment of suspension between each beat. [video here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjV59CPv6cM)

[neigh](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/neigh_1?q=neigh) (ngựa hí)

## Equiment

Hoof (plural hooves): móng ngựa

A [bridle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bridle) is a piece of equipment used to direct a horse. As defined in the Oxford English Dictionary, the "bridle" includes both the headstall that holds a bit that goes in the mouth of a horse, and the reins that are attached to the bit.  
bride

[stallion](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/stallion?q=stallion) (Noun): ngựa đực làm giống
gelding (Noun): a horse that has been castrated (thiến).
mare (Noun): a female horse or donkey.

[harness](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horse_harness)

groom

A [halter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Halter) or headcollar is headgear placed on animals used to lead or tie up livestock and, occasionally, other animals; it fits behind the ears (behind the poll), and around the muzzle. To handle the animal, usually a lead rope is attached. On smaller animals, such as dogs, a leash is attached to the halter.  
halter-neck is type of women dress.

A [stirrup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stirrup) (bàn đạp) is a light frame or ring that holds the foot of a rider, attached to the saddle by a strap, often called a stirrup leather.

[groom](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/groom_2): chải ngựa (verb) => bride (cô dâu) cưới bridegroom (chú rể) về để groom mái tóc dài như cái bờm ngựa.  
bridegroom
