## Eukarya domain

The two other domains are:

1. Archaea: cổ khuẩn
2. Bacteria: vi khuẩn

Eukarya domain consists of 4 kingdoms:

1. Protista (Sinh vật nguyên sinh)
2. Fungi
3. Plantae
4. Animalia
